Conclusion

The cannabis industry, like all newly established industries, will face many challenges. Rapid growth, new science discoveries, innovations in technology, processing and an evolving legal and regulatory environment will interact in predictable, and sometimes unpredictable, ways. This interaction will create setbacks and opportunities for anyone involved in the space. Our position is to embrace these changing conditions. That starts with a solid understanding of the current issues and opportunities within cannabis and DLT system technology.

With so much uncertainty and change, there are no established protocols or procedures, which means there is minimal resistance to adopting innovation. We are positioned to be on the leading edge, where innovation and technology create order out of chaos and uncertainty, in order to provide opportunities for new disruptive innovations and ultimately create a virtuous growth cycle in a rapidly expanding industry.
